Before You Book

Lesson Policy

  • Your preferred time is an inquiry, not a confirmed booking.
  • Lesson availability is confirmed after the coach, court time, and payment are arranged.
  • Unpaid lesson times are not reserved unless another arrangement is confirmed by Elevate Badminton.
  • Cancellations or rescheduling requests should be made at least 72 hours before the lesson time.
  • No-shows and late cancellations may lose their deposit or lesson payment.
  • Players should arrive ready to train and notify the coach as early as possible about schedule changes.
